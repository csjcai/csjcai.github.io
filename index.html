Jianrui Cai

I did my PhD at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I did my BSc and MSc at the Hunan University. I had worked as a research intern at DJI (Shenzhen, China), Google (Seattle, WA, US) and Apple (Cupertino, CA, US).

Research

I am interested in Image Processing, Computational Photography and Computer Vision.
Much of my research is about Deep Learning and Camera Pipeline.
(Image Restoration, Image Enhancement, Color Mapping and Image Compression)
