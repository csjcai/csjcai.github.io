I am a PhD student in Department of Computing (COMP), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, under the supervision of Prof. Lei Zhang.

Previously, I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. from College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering, Hunan University, in 2012 and 2015, respectively.
